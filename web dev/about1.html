Our Story

Founded in 2010, Wild Safari Adventures began with a simple mission: to share the incredible beauty of wildlife and nature with visitors from around the world while promoting conservation and sustainable tourism.

What started as a small family operation with just two safari vehicles has grown into one of the most respected safari companies in the region, offering a wide range of experiences while maintaining our commitment to small groups and personalized service.

Our founder, James Wilson, a former wildlife biologist, established the company after spending over a decade studying and documenting the behavior of wildlife in their natural habitats. His deep connection to the land and its inhabitants remains at the core of our company values.

Our Values & Mission

Conservation First

We believe that responsible tourism can play a vital role in wildlife conservation. A portion of all safari bookings goes directly to local conservation efforts and community development projects.

Our team works closely with wildlife protection agencies and local communities to ensure that our activities have minimal impact on the environment while maximizing benefits for both wildlife and people.

Education & Awareness

We're committed to creating meaningful connections between our guests and the natural world. Our guides are passionate educators who share their deep knowledge and respect for wildlife, helping to foster a greater understanding of conservation challenges and solutions.
